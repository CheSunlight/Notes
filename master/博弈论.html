Guessing Game

  • Everyone choose a number between [0, 100][0,\ 100][0, 100].
  • The one whose number is close to 23\frac{2}{3}32​ of the average win.
  • If you assume all other people choose the average of the range, i.e., 505050, then you may choose 50×23≈3350\times \frac{2}{3}\approx 3350×32​≈33. If you assume all other people have thought about this, i.e., they will choose 333333, then you may choose 33×23=2233\times \frac{2}{3}= 2233×32​=22, and so on.
  • Another prospective: the answer cannot exceed 100×23≈67100\times \frac{2}{3}\approx 67100×32​≈67, similarly we can repeat the process to get 67×23≈4567\times \frac{2}{3}\approx 4567×32​≈45, 45×23=3045\times \frac{2}{3}=3045×32​=30, and so on.
  • Both ways of thinking will derive 000 ultimately, but choosing 000 must not be the optimal strategy.
